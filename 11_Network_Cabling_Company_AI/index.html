    🎯 Infrastructure Components

    ✅ AI optimizes structured cabling: - Intelligent patch panel management - Smart trunk optimization - MDA (Main Distribution Area) monitoring - Fiber-optic performance analysis

    📊 Design Optimization

    ✅ AI ensures efficient layouts: - Cable pathway optimization - Cross-connect placement - Redundancy planning - Future expansion mapping

    AI analyzes traffic patterns → recommends optimal cable trunk placement for maximum efficiency.
